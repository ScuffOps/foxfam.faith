# Foxfam Portal Product Context

Foxfam Portal is Scuffox/Veri's private community and staff operating surface for care-vs-chaos community life: events, birthdays, shrine submissions, forum threads, relic progression, dashboards, and staff-only moderation support.

## Users

- Community members who need a warm, readable hub for events, forum participation, shrine activity, and earned progression.
- Mods, lead mods, and admins who need fast access to stream rules, mod manuals, command references, schedules, time tracking, tasks, updates, and member support tools.
- Veri/Scuffox as the chaotic center of gravity who needs support systems that acknowledge real energy, mood, time blindness, and stream unpredictability without becoming sterile or corporate.

## Product Goals

- Make community care feel earned, playful, and safe without flattening the tone into fake positivity.
- Keep staff operations practical: clear next actions, current updates, accountable task ownership, and role-gated tools.
- Support progression through recognition, roles, relics, charms, collectibles, and visible participation history.
- Preserve a tone of playful devotion rather than serious worship: warm underneath the chaos, teasing, self-aware, and never generic SaaS.

## Design Direction

- Use the existing dark glass/product UI system, readable typography, restrained panels, strong contrast, and compact admin-friendly density.
- Prefer clear modules, status chips, focused dashboards, and explicit role boundaries over decorative clutter.
- Keep Staff Ops feeling like a stream support cockpit: open tasks, on-duty coverage, updates, command references, handbook links, and pay/time summaries should be visible quickly.
- Copy can break the fourth wall and poke fun at scheduling chaos, but it should stay useful, kind, and legible.

## Anti-References

- Generic corporate dashboards, sterile wellness language, fake optimism, and bland community boilerplate.
- Serious religious framing for shrine language; the product should stay playful, self-aware, and opt-in.
- Hidden moderation tools or ambiguous permissions. Staff-only surfaces should make access boundaries obvious.

## Quality Bar

- Validate form and API payloads before writes.
- Keep role gates consistent across UI, client logic, and database policies.
- Protect private staff data, medication notes, time entries, and moderation context.
- Verify navigation, responsive layout, build output, and role-gated views before shipping.
